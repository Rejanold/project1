Raise Your Spirits shuttle is a 10 to 12 passenger (guests) Van that can be rented with a driver for the 1st TWO hours at $175 and $50 every hour after that. The first two hour fee is based on the pickup location being within 15 minutes of Lake Hallie, otherwise addition travel is extra. It is recommended that the best experience is accomplished with 10 persons (5 couples).
